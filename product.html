Aleuritic Acid

Aleuritic Acid (9, 10, 16-tri hydroxy palmitic acid) is obtained from shellac by saponification, is a unique acid containing three hydroxyl groups of which two are of adjacent carbon atoms.

Application:
  • Iso-Ambrettolide
  • Civeton
  • Dihydroxyacetone
  • Glucose Monolaurate
  • Plastics with good adhesive properties
Packaging:
  • 25 Kgs PE Bags with Inner Protection
  • 500 Kg FIBC Bag
